About the Author

Madison Draney is a college student pursuing music education. Since she could draw, she has been plastering her walls with derps, creating derp stories, and other general derpishness. She has put pen to paper to create Derp’s debut, “Derp”, with more adventures to come. She hopes that through her writing parents and children alike will have a lighthearted and humorous experience looking at the world from Derp’s one-eyed perspective.
